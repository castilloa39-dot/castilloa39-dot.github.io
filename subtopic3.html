Artificial Intelligence Applications to Personalized Dietary Recommendations: A Systematic Review
Site Author
 Xi Wang, Zhiyuan Sun, Hong Xue, and Ruopeng An

Abstract
 The article explains how artificial intelligence is being used to improve personalized dietary recommendations. Now, instead of relying on general nutrition guidelines, AI systems analyze vast amounts of data. This data includes eating habits, health information, and behaviors related to a person's lifestyle. This data allows the system to create recommendations tailored to each individual. The article explains that machine learning models improve over time because the more data they collect, the more accurate they become. Since the collected data identifies patterns between diet and health outcomes, AI helps by providing more effective and personalized advice. The article shows how artificial intelligence is transforming nutrition, making it more precise and individualized.

 Artificial Intelligence in Nutrition and Dietetics: A Comprehensive Review of Current Research
Site Author
 Gabriela Georgieva Panayotova
Abstract
 The article explains how artificial intelligence is transforming the field of nutrition and dietetics. AI is being used to improve dietary assessments, create nutritional plans, and, above all, support the management of chronic diseases. The article highlights how AI systems analyze data from various sources that includes food intake, health metrics, and wearable devices, then evaluate it to provide recommendations. The article also examines the use of AI in tools such as image-based food recognition and virtual entertainment systems, all with the aim of empowering users to manage their eating habits more effectively. The article emphasizes that while AI improves efficiency and accessibility, it should be used as a support tool, not a replacement for professionals.
